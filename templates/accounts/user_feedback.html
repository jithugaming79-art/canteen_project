Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse the menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using your wallet balance or other available payment methods.

How do I add money to my wallet?

Go to your Profile, then Wallet section. You can add money using any available payment method. The balance will be reflected instantly.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled before they start being prepared. Once the kitchen begins preparing your food, cancellation may not be possible.

What are the canteen timings?

The canteen operates Monday to Friday from 8AM to 6PM, and Saturday from 9AM to 3PM. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How do I track my order?

After placing an order, track its status in real-time from the Orders page. Statuses include Pending, Preparing, Ready for Pickup, and Collected.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are automatically processed to your wallet within 24 hours for cancelled or rejected orders. For any issues, submit feedback.
